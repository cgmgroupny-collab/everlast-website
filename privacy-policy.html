2 Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and project location when you request an estimate or contact us.

  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, referring URL, and approximate location derived from IP address.

  • Respond to inquiries, provide estimates, and schedule site visits.

  • Market additional services we think you’ll find useful — you can opt out at any time.

4 SMS Messaging Terms & Consent

Everlast General Contracting uses SMS (text messaging) to communicate with customers, leads, and prospects about contracting services. This section explains exactly what you can expect from our text messaging program, how you consent, and how to opt out at any time.

SMS Program Details

Everlast SMS Messaging Program

Program Name
Everlast General Contracting Customer & Lead Communications
Message Types
Appointment reminders, quote follow-ups, project status updates, scheduling confirmations, and occasional promotional offers for contracting services.
Frequency
Message frequency varies based on your project and account activity. Typical frequency: 2–6 messages per month.
Cost
Message & data rates may apply. Your carrier’s standard messaging rates apply to every message sent or received. Everlast does not charge a fee to use this SMS program.
Opt-In
By submitting your phone number through our contact form, requesting a quote, or replying YES or START to any Everlast message, you consent to receive SMS messages from Everlast General Contracting at the mobile number you provided. Consent is not a condition of purchase.
Opt-Out
You can cancel SMS messages at any time. Reply STOP to any message we send, or text STOP to the number you received the message from. After you send STOP, we will send you a confirmation message and stop sending SMS messages. If you want to join again, reply START.
Help
For help, reply HELP to any message, email everlastgcus@gmail.com, or call (508) 333-8415.
Carriers
Supported carriers include AT&T, Verizon Wireless, T-Mobile, Sprint, U.S. Cellular, and most major U.S. carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
Privacy
We will never share, sell, or rent your mobile number or SMS opt-in information to third parties or affiliates for their marketing purposes. Phone numbers collected for SMS messaging are used solely for Everlast communications.
Important: By providing your phone number on our website, contact forms, or lead forms and agreeing to our Privacy Policy, you expressly consent to receive calls and text messages from Everlast General Contracting — including messages sent using an automatic telephone dialing system — regarding your inquiry or project. Consent is not required to purchase services.

How We Verify SMS Opt-In

Everlast collects explicit written consent before sending marketing SMS messages. Opt-in consent is verified through one of the following methods:

  • Website form: you submit a contact or quote-request form with a checkbox that reads: “I agree to receive calls and text messages from Everlast General Contracting about my project. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel. Frequency varies. Consent is not a condition of purchase.”
  • Text keyword: you text a keyword (for example QUOTE or START) to our business number.
  • Verbal agreement: during a phone call or on-site visit, you verbally authorize us to text you — we log the date, time, and purpose of the consent.
  • Written agreement: you sign a contract, proposal, or estimate that includes an SMS communications clause.

All consent records are retained for at least four (4) years in compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and CTIA messaging guidelines.

5 How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: trusted vendors who help us run our business (scheduling software, CRM, accounting, payment processors, SMS platform). These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only to perform their services for us.
  • Subcontractors: if your project requires specialized trades (roofing, electrical, plumbing), we may share the minimum information needed to complete the job.
  • Legal compliance: when required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not share phone numbers collected for SMS with any third party for marketing purposes — ever.

6 Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Lead and contact records are typically retained for up to seven (7) years. SMS consent records are retained for at least four (4) years after opt-out to demonstrate compliance.

7 Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: request that we delete your information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt out of marketing: unsubscribe from emails via the link in any marketing email, or reply STOP to any SMS message.
  • Do Not Call: request that we stop calling your number for marketing purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, email everlastgcus@gmail.com or call (508) 333-8415. We will respond within 30 days.

8 Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information — including encryption in transit, access controls, and secure cloud storage. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data using industry-standard practices.

9 Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for adults aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (for example, social media profiles or partner pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing any information.

11 Changes to This Policy
